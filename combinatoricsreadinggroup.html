Drexel Combinatorics Reading Group

About

This is a small reading group focused on covering topics, basic and advanced, in algebraic combinatorics. Anyone is welcome to drop by, both graduate and undergraduate students (and faculty!?).

How this page looks belies how informal this is. In theory, Peter Mao and I run this. However, the words "run" and "and" are not well-defined.

Winter 2025

We meet every Friday from 4-5PM at Korman 245. Tentatively, we will be covering something from Stanley's Enumerative Combinatorics vol. 1 every week.
